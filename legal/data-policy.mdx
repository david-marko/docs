<h1 className="text-lg font-semibold"> 3. Legal Basis for Data Processing </h1>
We process personal data based on:
<ol>
    <li>Consent: Users must provide clear and explicit consent before their data is collected and processed.</li>
    <li>Contractual Necessity: Personal data is processed to fulfill contractual obligations, such as processing remittances.</li>

    <li>Legitimate Interest: In cases where processing is necessary for legitimate business interests, provided it does not override users' fundamental rights and freedoms.</li>

We may collect the following types of personal data:
<ul>

<h1 className="text-lg font-semibold"> 5. Purpose of Data Collection </h1>
Sarafa collects and processes personal data for the following purposes:
<ol>
    <li>To provide and improve our services, including processing and completing remittance transactions.</li>
    <li>To verify user identity and comply with legal obligations related to anti-money laundering (AML) and counter-terrorism financing (CTF).</li>

    <li>To maintain security, detect fraud, and prevent misuse of our platform.</li>
</ol>
<h1 className="text-lg font-semibold"> 6. Data Sharing and Third-Party Access </h1>
Sarafa will not share or disclose personal data to third parties unless:
<ol>
    <li>We have obtained the user's explicit consent.</li>
    <li>It is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, such as cooperating with law enforcement agencies.</li>
    <li>It is required to fulfill the terms of service, such as sharing data with payment processors or financial institutions for completing transactions.</li>
</ol>
All third-party partners and service providers are bound by contractual agreements to ensure the protection and confidentiality of personal data.
<h1 className="text-lg font-semibold"> 7. Data Security </h1>
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and integrity of personal data. This includes encryption, secure servers, firewalls, and regular security audits to protect against unauthorized access, loss, or damage.
<h1 className="text-lg font-semibold"> 8. Data Rentention </h1>
Sarafa will retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Data that is no longer needed will be securely deleted or anonymized.
