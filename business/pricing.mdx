# Why sarafa?

- Fast, Low-Cost, and Stable Transactions – Sarafa enable instant, affordable, and secure cross-border payments.
- Global Reach with Local Access – Supports multiple remittance corridors with a growing cash pickup and agent network.
- Developer-Friendly API – RESTful API with clear documentation, webhooks for real-time updates, and scalable infrastructure.

# What are Sarafa’s products/Services?

1. Sarafa API - Our API enables businesses to easily collect and make payments conveniently while mitigating foreign exchange risks by providing real time exchange rate and conversion for over 20 currencies. 
2. Virtual multi-currency business account- our b2b account ensures businesses manage their treasury easily across different jurisdiction without compromising on compliance or security. 
{/* 3. Agent App - giving Money transfer operators peace of mind by digitizing their operations through a single app.  */}
{/* 4. Customer App - Enabling retail clients send, receive, make payment and convert currencies. */}
3. Checkout - Enabling E-commerce companies collect payments and Humanitarian organizations collect donations.
